  • Chess board consist of 64 square boxes
  • Square boxes are of two color : black and white(alternately)
  • There are 32 chess pieces on chess board (16 pieces of black and 16 pieces of white)
  • Names of pieces are : KING, QUEEN, BISHOP, KNIGHT, ROOK AND PAWN
